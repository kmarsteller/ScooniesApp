Welcome to The Scoonies! But what IS a Scoonie?


Named after Ohio State standout and tournament darling, Scoonie Penn, The Scoonies brings an aspect of fantasy sports to March Madness. In the Scoonies, each player drafts a roster(s) of teams based on a limited budget, and that entry is called a Scoonie.

Selection:

Each participant starts with 200 points to spend on picking sets of teams, with each individual team's cost based on its seed value. 1-seeds cost 80 points, and the cost for each lower seed decreases by 5 points per step down: 2-seeds cost 75, 3-seeds cost 70, and 16-seeds cost just 5 points each. You must spend exactly 200 points on your selections. Get started and Make a Scoonie!


Scoring:

Teams can earn points in two ways: Advancement Points and Bonus Points.
Advancement Points: For each game one of your teams win, they earn Advacement Points equal to their seed number(1-16) multiplied by the round number they win in(1-6). For as long as a team survives, they accumulate the sum of all their Advancement Points.

Bonus Points: Bonus points are earned for reaching key milestones: +5 for making the Final Four(FF), +10 for reaching the Championship Game(CG), and +15 for winning it all(WIN).


Bonus Points Example: A 1-seed that goes all the way and wins the NCAA Championship would earn: (((1X1)+(1x2)+(1x3)+(1x4)+(1x5)+(1x6)) + (5 FF + 10 CG + 15 WIN)) = 51 points.

Teams that lose maintain their totals, but stop scoring points. The final score for a Scoonie is the sum of all points earned by your set of selections.

Winning

Entries are ranked by highest total score, and you can submit multiple entries with different team combinations. Each entry will cost you $20, payable to Proximo via Venmo or PayPal at the time you submit your Scoonie.
Prize distribution amounts will be announced before the tournament begins. Your payment status will be visible on the Standings page.

YOU MUST PAY BY THE START OF THE TOURNAMENT, OR YOUR ENTRY/ENTRIES WILL BE DELETED!
