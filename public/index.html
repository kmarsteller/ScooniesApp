How to Play

Welcome to The Scoonies, our NCAA Tournament selection game!


Team Selection: Each participant starts with 200 points to spend on picking teams, with costs based on seed values: 1 seeds cost 80 points, and each lower seed decreases by 5 points (2 seeds cost 75, 3 seeds cost 70, etc.) down to 16 seeds at just 5 points each. You must spend exactly 200 points on your selections.


Scoring: As the tournament progresses, your teams earn points equal to their seed number multiplied by the round they win in (Round of 32 = 1, Sweet 16 = 2, etc.), plus bonus points for reaching key milestones: +5 for making the Final Four, +10 for reaching the Championship game, and +15 for winning it all.

Entries are ranked by highest total score, and you can submit multiple entries with different team combinations.
